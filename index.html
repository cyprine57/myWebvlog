All About Cats

There are many different types of cats, and each cat can have their own distinct personality.

Their origin!

Cats have been with humans for thousands of years. They were first domesticated around 4,000 years ago in Egypt, where they were valued for protecting grain stores from rodents.

Over time, they became symbols of grace and companionship across many cultures.
